Project Arengea

Project Arengea is a new format for roleplaying tabletop games. Drawing inspiration from retro video games, the format seeks to recreate a dungeon crawling experience while removing complicated stats and constant checking. Simply put; the gameplay is purely RNG and the roleplay comes free. Gameplay is centered around character classes and enemy encounters that pit your party against a party made of monsters. You get base abilities from your class, but you can customize your class by equipping abilites learned from the enemies you defeat.

Quest for Arengea is a fictional roleplaying game serving as an example implementation of the Arengea format. All rules stated herein are just one possibility of using this format. It is encouraged to adapt all aspects of the rules to best suit your game whether you're recreating your favorite retro video game or playing a creation all your own. However, you're absolutely free to use Quest for Arengea as a base; and maybe one day with community development Quest for Arengea can become a reality.
